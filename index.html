About Us

Welcome to Elegant Head Lovers Salon, where Elegance Meets Expertise.

Conveniently located in the heart of Cape Town CBD, Elegant Head Lovers Salon is your ultimate all-in-one beauty hub. Under the expert direction of Susanne Luyinda, we pride ourselves on delivering premium hair, nail, and makeup services tailored entirely to you.

Whether you're looking for a fresh install, sleek natural ponytails, flawless lash extensions, professional makeup, or quality grooming for gents and kids, our talented team has you covered. Drop by our vibrant city center space—located perfectly between Clicks and Telkom opposite the train station—and let us treat you to the elegant experience you deserve.

Services We Offer

Hair Styling

We perform flawless custom installs, frontals, and sleek natural ponytails, As well as box braids, Knotless braids, cornrows for both Ladies and kids. For the gents, we have our expert barbers on standby, ready to perform miracle cuts and everyday styling tailored just for you.

Nail Care & Manicures

Professional manicures, pedicures, and stunning nail art designed to keep your hands and feet looking flawless (in collaboration with our dedicated nail team).

Flawless Makeup

Professional makeup styling for special events, photography, or when you just want to look and feel your absolute best. Including premium eyelash extensions and brow grooming to frame your face and give you an effortless, wake-up-and-go look.

The VIP Experience

With an added fee, Treat yourself to the ultimate pampering. Enjoy a completely customized, all-in-one beauty package with priority booking, complimentary refreshments, and a private, one-on-one styling session designed to make you feel like royalty.
